## Taxonomy

[[edit](/w/index.php?title=Bothriembryontidae&action=edit&section=1)]This family has been named Placostylidae and had no subfamilies (according to the [taxonomy of the Gastropoda by Bouchet & Rocroi, 2005](/wiki/Taxonomy_of_the_Gastropoda_(Bouchet_%26_Rocroi,_2005))).[[4]](#cite_note-Bouchet_2005-4)

Breure et al. (2010)[[5]](#cite_note-Breure_2010-5) moved *Prestonella* and *Bothriembryon* to Placostylidae.

Breure & Romero (2012)[[2]](#cite_note-Breure_2012-2) confirmed previous results from 2010 and they renamed Placostylidae to Bothriembryontidae.[[2]](#cite_note-Breure_2012-2)


- subfamily Bothriembryontinae Iredale, 1937
- subfamily Placostylinae Pilsbry, 1946 - for species from New Zealand and Melanesia[[2]](#cite_note-Breure_2012-2)

Prestonellinae was formally described as a new subfamily within Bothriembryontidae in 2016.[[6]](#cite_note-van_Bruggen_2016-6) Therefore three subfamilies are recognized in Bothriembryontidae:


- subfamily Bothriembryontinae Iredale, 1937
- subfamily Placostylinae Pilsbry, 1946
- Prestonellinae van Bruggen, Herbert & Breure, 2016[[6]](#cite_note-van_Bruggen_2016-6)
